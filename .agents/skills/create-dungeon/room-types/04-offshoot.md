# The Offshoot

## Description
A room that leads away from the main path, potentially to a secret or optional area.

## Purpose
- To provide choice and agency to the players.
- To house dangerous secrets or high-value rewards.
- To expand the sense of scale of the dungeon.

## Components
- **Access:** A narrow tunnel, a secret door, or a path requiring a specific ability.
- **Challenge:** Often more dangerous than the main path.
- **Reward:** Better loot or critical lore.
