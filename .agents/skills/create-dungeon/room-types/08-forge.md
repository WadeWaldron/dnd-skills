# The Forge

## Description
A utility room where things are created, repaired, or maintained.

## Purpose
- To provide a space for crafting or upgrading gear.
- To serve as a tactical objective (e.g., sabotage the enemy's production).
- To house fire-based creatures or hazards.

## Components
- **Tools:** Anvils, furnaces, alchemical labs, or woodworking benches.
- **Resources:** Ingots, raw materials, or specialized fuel.
- **Hazard:** High heat, toxic fumes, or mechanical dangers.
