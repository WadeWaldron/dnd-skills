# The Factions

## Description
A room that highlights the internal politics or conflicts between different groups in the dungeon.

## Purpose
- To offer non-combat solutions through diplomacy or manipulation.
- To make the dungeon feel like a living, breathing ecosystem.
- To introduce rivals or potential allies.

## Components
- **Evidence:** Signs of a struggle, borders/barricades, or distinct marking/flags.
- **NPCs:** Representatives of competing groups.
- **Conflict:** A disagreement over territory, loot, or leadership.
