# The Combat

## Description
A room focused on tactical engagement. While primarily combat-oriented, it should reflect the dungeon's theme and environment.

## Purpose
- To challenge the party's tactical prowess.
- To use up party resources (HP, spell slots).
- To establish the presence of specific creatures or factions.

## Components
- **Creatures:** Appropriate for the dungeon level and theme.
- **Environment:** To complicate or enhance the combat (cover, difficult terrain, hazards).
- **Loot:** Minor rewards found on enemies or in the room.
