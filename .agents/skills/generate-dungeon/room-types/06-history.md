# The History

## Description
A room dedicated to the lore and past of the dungeon or its inhabitants.

## Purpose
- To ground the dungeon in the world.
- To explain why the dungeon exists and what its original purpose was.
- To provide clues for puzzles or faction motivations.

## Components
- **Visuals:** Murals, statues, tapestries, or architecture.
- **Records:** Journals, stone tablets, or library shelves.
- **Discovery:** An Intelligence (History) or Intelligence (Investigation) check might reveal hidden meanings.
