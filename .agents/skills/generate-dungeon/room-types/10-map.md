# The Map

## Description
A room that provides clues or directions to *other* locations, adventures, or treasures outside of the current dungeon.

## Purpose
- To plant seeds for future sessions or campaigns.
- To expand the scope of the world beyond the current objective.
- To reward players with long-term goals.

## Components
- **The Information:** A star chart pointing to a fallen meteor, a map of a different continent, or a journal detailing a lost city.
- **Context:** The map might be part of an elaborate mural, a complex mechanical device, or hidden inside a statue.
- **Hook:** Information that makes the players say, "We need to go there next."
