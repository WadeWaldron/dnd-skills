# The Sidekick

## Description
A room containing a non-hostile (or potentially friendly) NPC or creature.

## Purpose
- To provide information or lore.
- To offer temporary assistance or a specific skill.
- To add emotional weight or roleplaying opportunities.

## Components
- **NPC:** A captive, a lost explorer, a sentient creature, or a spirit.
- **Interaction:** A social encounter, a rescue mission, or a negotiation.
- **Benefit:** Knowledge of traps ahead, a map segment, or help in the next combat.
