# The Foreshadowing

## Description
A room that provides hints or warnings about what lies ahead (especially the boss or final challenge).

## Purpose
- To build tension and anticipation.
- To allow the players to prepare for specific threats.
- To show the impact of the main villain or threat.

## Components
- **Evidence:** Claw marks, scorched walls, discarded items of previous victims, or distant sounds.
- **Atmosphere:** A change in temperature, smell, or ambient light.
- **Lore:** Scraps of paper, etchings, or warnings left by others.
