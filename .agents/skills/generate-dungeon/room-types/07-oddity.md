# The Oddity

## Description
A central, immovable feature that is often the primary reason the dungeon was built. It is an intrinsic part of the site that cannot be looted or moved.

## Purpose
- To define the dungeon's core identity or function.
- To provide a unique utility that encourages players to return.
- To act as a centerpiece for the dungeon's theme.

## Components
- **Central Feature:** A permanent planar portal, a celestial orrery, a "Chamber of Souls", or a primal mana nexus.
- **Utility:** A reason for players to interact with it, such as crafting, divination, or travel.
- **Narrative Weight:** The lore explaining why this feature is rooted in this specific location.
